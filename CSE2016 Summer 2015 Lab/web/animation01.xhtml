Animation 01

Write a JavaFX application that shows the following animation:
  • There are two squares of length L placed at two different x coordinates and the same y coordinate in the scene
      Values of L and the two x coordinates and y coordinate will be provided when your code will be tested
  • The squares will be moving towards each other at an uniform velocity, V
  • If the squares collide, they will start moving at -V velocity
  • If the squares collide with the walls of the scene, V will be negated again
